Our Story

A little garden, a lot of heart

Ammi ka Bagh isn't a farm — it's our backyard. A small patch of earth behind our home where every seed is sown by hand and every plant is known by name.

We believe in growing naturally. No shortcuts, no mass production — just sunshine, rich soil, patience, and care. Every fruit here is nurtured slowly, picked at its sweetest, and shared with joy.

This is a family passion project, tended with love across seasons — from the first spring seedlings to the last autumn harvest.
